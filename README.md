## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior 
using real transaction data from Olist, a 
Brazilian e-commerce marketplace, covering 
99,441 orders from September 2016 to 
October 2018.

The goal is to identify why customers don't 
return, segment them by value, and provide 
actionable business recommendations.

## Key Insights

**1. Platform Loyalty Problem**
97% of customers made only one purchase 
and never returned — not due to poor 
experience (avg review: 4.16/5) but 
due to lack of platform engagement.

**2. At Risk Segment**
22,311 customers with R$3.7M historical 
revenue have been inactive for 394 days 
on average — highest priority for 
win-back campaigns.

**3. Champions Are 8.6x More Valuable**
Champions generate R$122 avg CLV vs R$14 
for Lost customers — protecting this 
segment is critical.

**4. R$2.07M CLV Uplift Possible**
Converting At Risk + Cant Lose Them 
segments to Champion level represents 
R$2,077,785 in additional lifetime value.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. **Launch Loyalty Program** — Olist Points 
   system to incentivize repeat purchases

2. **At Risk Win-Back Campaign** — Immediate 
   targeted emails with discount offers

3. **VIP Program for Champions** — Free 
   shipping, early access, birthday offers

4. **Post-Purchase Email Sequence** — 
   5-email automated flow after every order

5. **High-Frequency Category Expansion** — 
   Pet food, beauty refills, baby products
